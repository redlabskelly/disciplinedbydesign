"Your life does not get better by chance. It gets better by change."

Change requires disciplne, and discipline requires a plan. A plan can't just be an idea. You need support, accountability, and clear and defined goals. At Discipline by Design, we help you meet your personal goals, team goals, and academic and professional goals.

College Consulting

College is one of the biggest decisions of your life. Students require guidance for navigating the college application process, SAT/ACT studying, financial aid and scholarships; and that's all before selecting a college and degree program.

Disciplined by Design helps students at all levels of high school to understand their needs in selecting a university, and figuring out the pieces they need to get accepted to their dream school. We offer goals and career assessments, including a complete Birkman assessment and consultation to develop a list of colleges that meet each student's needs. We can also provide test prep support, application and financial aid assistance, and interview and resume help.

Personal Development

People should never stop changing, learning, and creating new goals. We offer the tools and resources you need to not only set goals, but understand the best way for you to meet those goals. Every person has different motivations and requires different incentives, but everyone can craft willpower and create change. We just help you understand the strategies to make you your best self.

Team Building

Even when we are aware of our own needs, it can often be difficult to understand the needs of others. When we work in teams, communication is essential, and it can break down quickly and easily. Taking the time to understand the people on your team, and the best strategies to effectively work together can save valuable time and energy. It can take years to understand your employees or your bosses needs. Or with Disciplined by Design, it can take a few hours.

Additionally, we offer team building exercises and workshops that help create an environment that is conducive to your culture.
