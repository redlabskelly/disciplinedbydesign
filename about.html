"Everyone must choose one of two pains. Discipline or regret."

Kelly McCormick is the founder of Discipline by Design. Kelly works at the University of Houston, in one of the highest ranked entrepreneurship programs in the country, the Wolff Center for Entrepreneurship. She helps UH-affiliated students, faculty and alumni launch startups and small businesses, and has experience helping students and professionals understand how to set and achieve goals. She also has experience helping individuals work together in teams.

Before the University of Houston, Kelly received her law degree from Tulane University, and a certificate in the fields of negotation, mediation and arbitration. She also has a degree in Finance from Miami University. Throughout her time at both Tulane and Miami, Kelly served in the administration and worked with both professors and students.

Beyond working with Kelly, she assembles the best mentors and contacts for her clients to help them meet their personal and professional goals.
